Nothingness

Joseph Scallan

There is respite in the nothingness. An endless existentialism. Neither tranquility nor pain is present here. Bask or despair, it matters not. Before you stands the timeless void. Somewhere is nowhere. Something is nothing. YOU are someone and no one. Yet you continue to exist and defy OUR laws. THERE IS NOTHING HERE, THERE, OR NOWHERE FOR YOU!!!!! And what am I you ask? They could be the voices in your head. Everything is we. I even could be the shade with wings behind you coming to take you somewhere or nowhere.
